Seafood

Beaches & sights
Golden sand, cinematic forts and turtle-nesting calm.
Candolim Beach4.5 ★
15 min

A long golden arc, noticeably quieter than Baga. Excellent shacks, reliable water sports and cinematic sunsets, with Aguada Fort a short walk away.

Chapora Fort4.4 ★
15 min

A 17th-century Portuguese bastion above Vagator with sweeping Arabian Sea views — most magnificent in the last hour before sunset.

Morjim Beach4.4 ★
25–30 min

Serene and rarely crowded — a protected Olive Ridley turtle nesting site with yoga retreats, quiet cafés and luminous sunsets.
